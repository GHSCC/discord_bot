Advisor Commands
.assignassignment   : used by the advisors to give assignments to the students role
.assignmentprogress : used by the mods to check on what each student has submitted as their answer
.start_meeting       : starts a meeting and allows the students to join in
.end_meeting         : ends a meeting recording the time that all the students stayed within the meeting
.add_question        : adds a question into the question bank, the question bank will be renewed every single week
